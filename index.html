Who We Are

A Sabah Company Building Malaysia's Digital Future

TechnoMoments began as an operator in two of Sabah's most vital industries — palm oil agriculture and solar energy. Our deep roots in Borneo's land and community give us a perspective few technology companies possess: we don't just build software, we understand the physical reality of the industries we serve.

Today, we are evolving into a full-spectrum technology solutions provider — bringing agentic AI to SMEs, precision intelligence to agriculture, and digital transformation platforms to government agencies across Malaysia.

AgriTech & GovTech

Data Intelligence for Agriculture & Government

Leveraging deep expertise in Sabah's agricultural sector and established government relationships, we design technology platforms that transform how ministries and agri-businesses operate — from field-level sensor data to policy-grade analytics.

IoT & Sensor Integration

Aggregate data from field sensors, weather stations, and monitoring hardware into unified operational dashboards — eliminating data silos at the source.

Satellite & GIS Analytics

Sentinel-2 NDVI imagery, spatial analytics, and GIS systems integrated with ground-truth data for crop health monitoring and land-use planning.

AI Yield Forecasting

Machine learning models trained on local agricultural data — predicting yields, identifying risks, and informing supply planning decisions with precision.

Supply Chain & Marketplace

Farm-to-market logistics platforms, digital marketplace systems, and cold chain monitoring — reducing post-harvest losses and middleman dependency.

Agricultural Intelligence Platform

We are building towards becoming Sabah's leading AgriTech solutions provider — developing integrated platforms that combine IoT sensor networks, satellite imagery analytics, AI-driven yield forecasting, and end-to-end supply chain management. Our goal is to empower farmers, agri-businesses, and government agencies with the data intelligence they need to make better decisions, reduce waste, and maximise yield.
